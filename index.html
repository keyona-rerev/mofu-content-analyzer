What a content capsule actually does

A content capsule is the set of content assets that carry a prospect from not knowing they have a problem to signed — without you having to cover everything live, on a call, every single time. It's leverage: write it once, send it as many times as you have prospects who need it.

It sits in the middle of the funnel — after someone's shown some kind of interest (a discovery call, a demo request, a form fill) but before they've decided. That middle stretch is where most of it gets won or lost, and it splits into two jobs:

Two jobs, one capsule: nurture is what keeps a prospect warm and moving when a live conversation didn't end in a locked calendar invite. Conversion is what finally tips someone from interested to signed — closing the last doubts a call alone couldn't close. Each of the five categories below exists to do one part of that:

SymptomOpens a nurture sequence for someone with no urgency yet — builds the case that a problem exists at all.
SolutionMid-sequence, for someone comparing options — teaches them how to evaluate, before you're in the running.
Value PropFor someone deciding between you and an alternative — makes the direct case for why you.
ProofCloses the last doubt before signature — someone else's word, with a number attached.
ProductFinal step before conversion — removes the remaining unknowns so day one has no surprises.
