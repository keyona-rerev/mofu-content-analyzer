T029 · MOFU Content Analyzer

Does your content do its job?

Enter a site's blog or content URL. This crawls it, classifies every substantive page into one of five content-capsule jobs, scores each 0–100, and checks the 3-piece minimum per job.

Reads whatever the URL's nav links to (blog index, product pages, etc.) — no login-gated or private content.
Crawling and classifying — this can take 10–20 seconds…
